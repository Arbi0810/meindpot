Who We Are

A slice of New York

Pizza so good, you’ll swear you’re in New York. That’s because we moved from New York in 1975 and have been serving Los Angeles a slice of the big apple ever since. Generous portions, the freshest ingredients, and an authentic New York atmosphere are just some of the reasons Joe Peeps is as famous as some of our biggest fans. Drop in and see for yourself what makes our pizza the best in LA.
